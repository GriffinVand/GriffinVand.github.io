Potato Room

Role: Sole Creator

Environment art based on 2D concept art

About

This project was done for an 3D Environment Art class. I had to make a fully modeled environment based off of specifically 2D concept art All modeling was done in Blender and all texturing was done in Substance 3D Painter or Substance 3D Designer. Assembled and rendered in Unreal Engine 5.
