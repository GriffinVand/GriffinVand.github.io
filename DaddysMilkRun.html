About

Made in UE5, Daddy's Milk Run was my solo submission for a 48hr game jam with the theme "Break through". The goal is to drive down a road and eventually reach your family to save them from an alien threat. Aliens will spawn incessently along the player's path in larger and larger amounts as they get closer to the end. Thus, the player must "break through" the alien obstacles via a top-mounted machine gun, two missile launchers, and their driving abilities without allowing the car to be destroyed.

Assets

The following assets were made by me. All others were outsourced:

  • Models: Player and alien gun
  • Textures: Player, alien gun, facecam frame
  • VFX: Bullet and blood effects
  • Sound: All player sounds
